A facebook game is circulating where, if nominated, you are asked to post a cover of music albums that were most influential to you over 10 consecutive days, in the process nominating someone else to do the same. For wrong or right, I can't help but feel it a bit intrusive to inundate people's feeds with things they neither care about or can benefit much from (given that it's only the cover, they can't search for the album if they wanted to). However, I did find the premise of the game completely enjoyable. Being someone who consumes music on a daily basis, some albums have stuck with me to the point that they are part of my identity just like the color of my eyes or anything else that defines a person. 

So for better or for worse, I decided to blog about it - if not for you (the reader) then for me. I often go back and analyze this question so why not have it on the internet where I can go back and read it, analyze it, and remember the great albums.

Without further ado, here are the top 10 albums that have influenced my taste in music (no particular order).
